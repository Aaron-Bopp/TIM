#### <s class="topic-title">[[Basal Ganglia]]</s>

Situated at the base of the forebrain and top of the midbrain. Basal ganglia are strongly interconnected with the [[cerebral cortex]], [[thalamus]], and [[brainstem]], as well as several other brain areas. 

The basal ganglia are associated with a variety of functions, including control of voluntary motor movements, procedural learning, habit learning, conditional learning, eye movements, [[cognition]], and emotion.
