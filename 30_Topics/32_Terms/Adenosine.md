##### <s class="topic-title">[[Adenosine]]</s>

One of four nucleoside building blocks to [[DNA]] and [[RNA]]. Adenosine and adenosine receptors play important roles in regulating wakefulness and sleep

Adenosine partially comes from recycling ATP

When administered Adenosine can give the [[sense of impending doom|feeling of impending doom]]
