#### <s class="topic-title">[[GAD]]</s>
`=this.file.aliases`

People with **generalized [[anxiety]] disorder** (GAD) display excessive [[anxiety]] or worry, most days for at least 6 months, about a number of things such as personal health, work, social interactions, and everyday routine life circumstances. The [[fear]] and anxiety can cause significant problems in areas of their life, such as [[human behavior|social interaction]]s, school, and work.
^[[[notes - Diagnosing Anxiety]]]
